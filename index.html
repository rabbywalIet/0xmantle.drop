Mantle Journey Season Alpha Rewards FAQ

Who is eligible to claim Mantle Journey rewards?

Users must have (1) passed sybil and bot checks, (2) earned a minimum of 200 MJ Miles in Season Alpha and (3) completed a minimum of one (1) social attestation via your MJ profile before Jan 15, 2024, 11:59PM UTC if potential bot transactions are identified with your wallet address, to be eligible for rewards.

When is the claim period for Mantle Journey rewards

User can claim their rewards from Jan. 29, 2024, 8AM UTC to Feb. 28, 2024, 11:59PM UTC. Please note that eligible reward recipients must claim their rewards during the claim period or have their rewards forfeited. Any unclaimed rewards will be returned to Mantle Treasury. Once claimed, users can check for their rewards in their wallets.

How does the Sybil Check work ?

The Sybil check detects:

  1. Addresses transferring funds in a cluster of more than 3 addresses with high-confidence
  2. Addresses with similar activities or bot behavior
  3. Addresses that are funded from the same source, such as a bridge so on
  4. Other potential sybil checks with high-confidence Sybil addresses identified by X-explore/0xScope on Ethereum and multiple Ethereum layer-2 chains

Subaccounts will not be automatically detected as a sybil.

How will the rewards be calculated for MJ miles?

It is calculated based on (Eligible MJ Miles per User / Total Eligible MJ Miles of All Users) x 15,000,000 MNT. Eligible MJ Miles refers to any MJ Miles that have fulfilled the conditions as outlined under claim eligibility in the first question.

Moonshot Rewards FAQ

Who is eligible to claim Moonshot rewards?

Users who have a Citizens of Mantle NFT upgraded with a Moonshot Protocol add-on trait at snapshot on Feb. 1, 2024. Please note that you will only be allocated a single reward per protocol add-on trait (successfully upgraded with a Citizens of Mantle NFT), even if you have multiple NFTs upgraded with the same add-on trait.

If I buy a Citizens of Mantle NFT upgraded with a Moonshot Protocol add-on trait, will I be eligible for rewards?

You are eligible as long as you hold a Citizens of Mantle NFT upgraded with any of the 8 Moonshot Protocol add-on traits before the snapshot on Feb. 1, 2024. However, please note that even if you hold two different NFTs upgraded with the same add-on trait, one wallet address can only receive a maximum of a single allocation of protocol token reward to that corresponding trait. Those who sell their upgraded NFTs and do not hold them over snapshot will not be eligible for protocol token rewards from Moonshot.

When is the claim period for Moonshot rewards?

For all protocol token rewards from Moonshot Protocols except for KTX.Finance, Lendle, FusionX Finance and Minterest, eligible users can claim their respective rewards within 30 days from Feb. 21, 2024 onwards. Once claimed, users can check for their rewards in their wallet. Failure to claim reward(s) during the claim period will result in reward(s) being forfeited, and any unclaimed rewards will be returned to protocol teams. For remaining Moonshot Protocols, protocol token rewards will be distributed by the projects as mentioned in Moonshot weekly guides. Please view more details here.

How can I get a Citizens of Mantle NFT?

For users who have fully completed a minimum of 6 Moonshot Protocol quests during the Moonshot period, they will receive their complimentary Citizens of Mantle NFT within 7 working days. Users may also purchase and bid for a Citizens of Mantle NFT via secondary marketplaces such as Mintle.
